Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the construction cost calculator?
Our construction cost calculator provides estimates with approximately 85-90% accuracy based on current market rates, material costs, and labor charges in Karnataka. For a precise quote, we recommend scheduling a free consultation with our experts who will visit your site and provide a detailed, itemized estimate.
What factors affect construction costs the most?
The main factors influencing construction costs are: (1) Package selection and material quality, (2) Location and local regulations, (3) Plot dimensions and built-up area, (4) Number of floors and structural complexity, (5) Construction type (residential/commercial/industrial), (6) Design specifications and architectural features, (7) Soil conditions and foundation requirements.
How long does it take to get a detailed estimate after using the calculator?
After submitting your details through our calculator, our team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss your requirements in detail. We typically provide a comprehensive, itemized estimate within 2-3 working days after understanding your specific needs and visiting the site if required.
Do you offer construction services across Karnataka?
Yes, MG Buildcon provides construction services across Karnataka including Bangalore, Mysuru, Hubli, Mangalore, and surrounding areas. We have successfully completed projects in both urban and semi-urban locations throughout the state.
What is included in your construction packages?
Our packages include complete construction from foundation to finishing. The Standard Package includes basic finishes, Premium adds better quality materials, Premium Pro includes premium fixtures, Luxury includes high-end specifications, and Ultra Luxury offers custom luxury finishes. All packages include structural construction, electrical, plumbing, plastering, painting, and flooring.
Can I modify the estimate after getting the initial calculation?
Absolutely! The online calculator provides an initial estimate. Once you connect with our team, we can customize every aspect of the estimate based on your specific requirements, design changes, material preferences, and any additional features you want to include in your project.
