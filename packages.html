Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Silver Package?

The Silver Package includes concept floor plan development, floor plan changes, final floor plan, 3D elevation, working drawings (floor plan), and 2D elevation details. This package is ideal for clients who need basic architectural services for approval and construction.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The timeline depends on the package selected and the complexity of the project. Typically, Silver Package projects take 2-3 weeks, Gold Package projects take 3-4 weeks, and Diamond Package projects take 4-6 weeks for completion of drawings.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes, you can upgrade your package at any stage of the project. The cost will be adjusted accordingly based on the work already completed and the additional services required.

Do you provide site visits with all packages?

Site visits are included only in the Diamond Package. For Silver and Gold Packages, initial site measurement is included, but ongoing site visits are not part of the package.

Are the prices fixed or negotiable?

The prices mentioned are approximate and for standard projects. Final pricing may vary based on specific requirements, site conditions, and project complexity. We offer customized quotes after understanding your specific needs.
